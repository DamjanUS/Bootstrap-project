OUR STORY
“To Ralph the Greatest Pizza Man in the World” — Mickey Mantle, Hall of Famer Ralph and Deborah Lanza savored every slice of life, starting with family, baseball and pizza. Continuing a generational love affair with pizza making that started in Italy with Lanza’s grandfather, Michele, the chef to King Emmanuel III, the pair enjoyed great fun in the family-run pizzeria in NYC which first opened in 1904.
At Lanza’s, both Ralph and Deborah enjoyed making pizza for Fantasy Camps, Hall of Famers, and New Yorkers alike with Ralph gaining an honorary title “the greatest pizza man in the world” by none other than professional baseball player and Commerce Comet, Mickey Mantle. Reflecting on their success following Ralph’s passing in 2002, Deborah had an idea to shake up the pizza industry and immortalize the Lanza pizza legacy. Pizza Saver LLC’s innovative and exciting new product adds tremendously to the pizza experience and the enjoyment it brings to all. Kneaded with the very same love and passion invested into Lanza’s, the Spice Shaker Pizza Saver has significant business potential, providing a unique and competitive edge to grow your brand’s market share, increase revenue exponentially, and go viral, generating huge publicity.
